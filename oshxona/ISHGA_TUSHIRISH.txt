Taste of Uzbekistan – saytni ishga tushirish
============================================

1) Terminalda (yoki CMD/PowerShell):
   cd C:\Users\User\Desktop\oshxona
   npm start

   Yoki: start.bat faylini ikki marta bosing (ochiladi va serverni ishlatadi).

2) Brauzerda oching:
   http://localhost:4000          – asosiy sayt
   http://localhost:4000/admin.html – admin panel

3) To'xtatish: terminalda Ctrl+C

Agar "npm start" ishlamasa: avval "npm install" ni ishga tushiring.
